<p> Medicare Program 
<h2> Also available at Mindspan - Dementia care covered by Medicare - at no cost to you 
<p> The CMS GUIDE Model provides coordinated, team-based care for people living with dementia — plus practical support for caregivers. $0 copays, 24/7 access, and up to $2,500 in annual respite benefits. 
<a> Learn more about GUIDE → 
<div> 
<p> Who qualifies for GUIDE? 
<div> 
<div> 
<p> Has a dementia diagnosis (Alzheimer’s, Lewy Body, vascular, or other) 
<div> 
<div> 
<p> Enrolled in Medicare Part B (Original Medicare or supported Advantage plans) 
<div> 
<div> 
<p> Has a caregiver involved in day-to-day support 
<div> 
<div> 
<p> Lives in Massachusetts or California (our current GUIDE service areas) 
<div> Coverage and eligibility vary by region and payer. We verify benefits and educate families during onboarding.

<h2> The future of cognitive health 
<div> 
<div> 
<div> 
<div> 
<h3> Your own digital twin 
<div> Imagine a personal digital version of your brain —a "digital twin"—helping your care team truly understand and support your cognitive health. This innovative technology creates a detailed, personalized model of your unique brain function from your health records and assessments. It helps us pinpoint your cognitive strengths and challenges, then tailors your care precisely with personalized strategies. Trained on real-patient data from over 70,000 Alzheimers patients and 20 years of studies.
